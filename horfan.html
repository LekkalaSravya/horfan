ABOUT :

If you’re looking for books that create atmosphere, grab people's attention and generate emotional responses, then it’s hard to go past horror/fantasy stories. Love them or hate them, most people harbour a sort of morbid fascination of horror/fantasy stories. In fact, horror is such a high interest genre that it is often used to tempt reluctant readers into developing an interest in books and reading.where as fantasy may not be real but Readers want to be drawn into a setting that immerses them in a different time and place.Elements that are imaginative are wondrous and exciting, especially if it's not something you would have imagined yourself.

DRACULA

Dracula comprises journal entries, letters, and telegrams written by the main characters. It begins with Jonathan Harker, a young English lawyer, as he travels to Transylvania. Harker plans to meet with Count Dracula, a client of his firm, in order to finalize a property transaction. When he arrives in Transylvania, the locals react with terror after he discloses his destination: Castle Dracula. Though this unsettles him slightly, he continues onward. The ominous howling of wolves rings through the air as he arrives at the castle. When Harker meets Dracula, he acknowledges that the man is pale, gaunt, and strange. Read More

The Shining is set in Colorado in the 1970s. It centres on the Torrance family: husband Jack, wife Wendy, and their five-year-old son, Danny. At the beginning of the novel, Jack is hired as the caretaker of the remote Overlook Hotel for the winter offseason.
